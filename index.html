Overview

This is a detailed report on the dissection of the Onn Mini Rugged Speaker. The goal of this project was to identify the key components and their functions, explore the construction, and estimate the overall cost of the device.

Dissection Process

The dissection of the Onn Mini Rugged Speaker involved opening the casing, removing internal components, and documenting each piece. The goal was to understand the physical construction and organization of components inside the speaker.

Chips and Components Analysis

IP4056 - Battery Charger Chip

This chip is used to manage the charging process for the speaker's battery. It is crucial for maintaining battery health and ensuring safe charging.

LTK5302 - High-Quality Audio Amplifier Chip

The LTK5302 is the audio amplifier used to drive the speaker. It ensures high-quality sound output and optimal power usage.

ATS2853 - Bluetooth Chip

The ATS2853 is responsible for Bluetooth connectivity, enabling wireless communication between the speaker and other devices.

Pricing

The Onn Mini Rugged Speaker is priced at $10 at Walmart. This low price is an indicator of the cost-effective components used in the design.
