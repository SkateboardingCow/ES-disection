5. Product Analysis

Here’s an analysis of the key components of the Onn Mini Rugged Speaker:

Key Chips on PCB

  • IP4056: Battery Charger Chip (16 resistors/capacitors)
  • LTK5302: High-Quality Audio Amplifier Chip (18 resistors/capacitors)
  • ATS2853: Bluetooth Chip (24 resistors/capacitors)

Resistor of Interest

The 4R7 resistor is a key component. It is present in the following areas:

  • 16 resistors/capacitors next to the IP4056 Battery Charger Chip
  • 18 resistors/capacitors in the LTK5302 zone
  • 24 resistors/capacitors in the ATS2853 Bluetooth chip zone

Let's estimate the cost:

  • Cost of 4R7 resistor: $0.10 each (estimated)
  • Total for all resistors (58 resistors total): $5.80

Additional Notes:

  • Three prong component: Transistor
  • Black components: Resistors
  • Brown components: Capacitors
