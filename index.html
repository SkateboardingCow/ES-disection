5. Product Analysis

- 16 resistors/capacitors near IP4056 (battery charger chip) - 18 resistors/capacitors in LTK5302 zone (audio amplifier) - 24 resistors/capacitors near ATS2853 (Bluetooth chip)

Notable components: - “4R7” is a resistor - The 3-prong component is a transistor - Black components = resistors - Brown components = capacitors

Example cost breakdown: If one resistor costs $0.01 and there are 58 of them, the total is ~$0.58.
