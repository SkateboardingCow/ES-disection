1. Introduction

My name is Cody Swann and I am a junior at Mississippi State University in Computer Engineering. This is my dissection project for Embedded Systems (ECE 4724).

The device I have chosen for this project is the Onn Mini Rugged Speaker. I selected this device due to my interest in embedded systems and how small, portable devices like this speaker utilize compact electronics to function efficiently.
