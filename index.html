1. Introduction

My name is Cody Swann, and I am a junior at Mississippi State University studying Computer Engineering. This is my dissection project for Embedded Systems (ECE 4724/6724).

The device I chose is the Onn Mini Rugged Speaker. I selected this device because I enjoy learning how embedded systems power the everyday electronics we rely on. I'm especially interested in how compact audio devices like this deliver both durability and functionality on a budget. This project gave me the opportunity to explore the hardware behind Bluetooth connectivity, audio amplification, and portable power management—all areas that tie into my interests in engineering and consumer tech.

5. Product Analysis

- 16 resistors/capacitors near IP4056 (battery charger chip)
- 18 resistors/capacitors in LTK5302 zone (audio amplifier)
- 24 resistors/capacitors near ATS2853 (Bluetooth chip)

Notable components:
- “4R7” is a resistor
- The 3-prong component is a transistor
- Black components = resistors
- Brown components = capacitors

Example cost breakdown:
If one resistor costs $0.01 and there are 58 of them, the total is ~$0.58.
