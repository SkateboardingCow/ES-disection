1. Introduction

My name is Cody Swann and I am a junior at Mississippi State University in Computer Engineering. This is my dissection project for Embedded Systems (ECE 4724/6724).

I chose the Onn Mini Rugged Speaker as my dissection subject because of my love for audio devices and how embedded systems play a key role in making compact, portable electronics function effectively. This project combines my technical interests with hands-on exploration of real-world consumer products.
