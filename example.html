Overview

The Onn Mini Rugged Speaker is a compact Bluetooth speaker built for durability and portability. This dissection project aims to analyze its internal components, functionality, and market value to gain insight into its design and manufacturing choices. We will look at the speaker’s performance, the chips used, and the overall construction that makes it such an affordable yet high-performing device.

Dissection Process

In this section, we describe the process of carefully disassembling the Onn Mini Rugged Speaker, documenting each step of the journey, and analyzing its internal components. This includes examining the speaker's internal circuitry, chips, and the assembly/disassembly process.

Chips and Components Analysis

Bluetooth Chip

The Bluetooth chip is crucial for wireless communication, allowing the speaker to connect seamlessly to Bluetooth-enabled devices.

Audio Amplifier Chip

This component boosts the audio signal, ensuring that the speaker produces loud and clear sound, even at high volumes.

Battery Management Chip

This chip controls the charging and discharging cycles of the speaker’s internal battery, ensuring it operates efficiently and safely.

Pricing

The Onn Mini Rugged Speaker is priced at approximately $9.88 at Walmart, making it an affordable option for budget-conscious consumers looking for quality audio in a durable package.
